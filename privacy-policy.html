Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dink On ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our Pinterest and Etsy integration tool.

1. Information We Collect

This app is a personal automation tool. It accesses your Pinterest and Etsy account data (such as product listings, board information, and basic analytics) solely to automate Pin creation and scheduling for your own shop.

2. How We Use Information

Data accessed through Pinterest and Etsy APIs is used only to:

  • Create and schedule Pins from Etsy product listings
  • Retrieve basic reporting data to evaluate Pin performance

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your own device. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers controlled by this app.

4. Third-Party Services

This app interacts with the Pinterest API and Etsy API. Use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.
